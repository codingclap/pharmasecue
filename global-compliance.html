Global Compliance

Pharmaceutical and food safety regulations exist globally to combat counterfeit drugs and maintain quality and safety standards. Our serialization and traceability solutions are designed to keep up with trade regulations worldwide. This enables our clients to seamlessly conform with various global mandates of countries including the United States, European Union, India, Brazil, South Korea, Saudi Arabia and Russia.

We pride ourselves on our on-demand support and after-service. This means that you can rely on our experts for a detailed walk-through to better understand the serialization mandates. Browse through our country-specific guides below for an overview.

Brazil (ANVISA)
  • Serialization Status: Required
  • Code Format - 2D Data Matrix
  • GTIN - AI (01)
  • ANVISA Registry Number
  • Expiration Date - AI (17)
  • Batch/Lot Number - AI (10)
  • Serial Number - AI (21)
India (iVEDA)
  • Serialization Status: Required
  • Code Format - 2D Data Matrix
  • GTIN - AI (01)
  • Expiration Date - AI (17)
  • Batch/Lot Number - AI (10)
  • Serial Number - AI (21)
India (Top 300)

Russia (MDLP)
  • Serialization Status: Required
  • Code Format - 2D Data Matrix
  • GTIN - AI (01)
  • Unique Serial Number - AI (21)
  • Key (4 Digit Code) - AI (91)
  • Signature - AI (92)
European Union (FMD)
  • Serialization Status: Required
  • Code Format - 2D Data Matrix
  • GTIN - AI (01)
  • Expiration Date - AI (17)
  • Batch Number - AI (10)
  • Serial Number - AI (21)
  • National Health Number - Where Necessary
US (DSCSA)
  • Serialization Status: Required
  • Code Format - 2D Data Matrix
  • GTIN - AI (01)
  • ANVISA Registry Number
  • Expiration Date - AI (17)
  • Batch/Lot Number - AI (10)
  • Serial Number - AI (21)
