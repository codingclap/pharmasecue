psID® Suite of Software

psID® TruTrakTM

psID TruTrak is PharmaSecure’s one-stop suite of hardware and software solutions for intelligent supply chain traceability. TruTrak ensures product visibility along the supply chain right from the point of manufacturing to the point of sale. Its definitive solutions combined with precise implementation and ongoing support help you effectively protect your most important asset – your brand.

Our Software Offering

A complete L1-L5 solution that caters to all packaging levels, enabling efficient traceability and insights into your supply chain. Our products are built using a modular approach for different global mandates and are compatible with most hardware in the market.

Manufacturing

psID® Manage is a world-class serialization and regulatory compliance solution. It enables users to request, fulfil, allocate, print and reconcile codes with role-based access control. This includes a web-based dashboard to monitor lines and audit trails.
