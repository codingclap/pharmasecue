psID® Suite of Software

psID® TruTrakTM

psID TruTrak is PharmaSecure’s one-stop suite of hardware and software solutions for intelligent supply chain traceability. TruTrak ensures product visibility along the supply chain right from the point of manufacturing to the point of sale. Its definitive solutions combined with precise implementation and ongoing support help you effectively protect your most important asset – your brand.

Our Software Offering

A complete L1-L5 solution that caters to all packaging levels, enabling efficient traceability and insights into your supply chain. Our products are built using a modular approach for different global mandates and are compatible with most hardware in the market.

Manufacturing

psID® Manage is a world-class serialization and regulatory compliance solution. It enables users to request, fulfil, allocate, print and reconcile codes with role-based access control. This includes a web-based dashboard to monitor lines and audit trails. In addition, it provides a variety of input and output options for flexible integration with industry-standard hardware and software. Our software also lets manufacturers use third-party codes.

psID® Imprint is specifically designed to connect in real-time to printers and scanning devices. With this centralized station, the line setup is reduced to a minimum, ensuring the integrity of each serial number.

psID® Aggregate is a fully customizable solution where every item, case and pallet are assigned a serial number and a parent-child relationship is created between packaging levels for efficiency in shipping and tracking.

psID® Shipment is our one-stop solution for all shipment related activities. It enables seamless printing and re-printing of codes, de-aggregation or re-aggregation of parent-child relationships, as well as uploading of XML files.

psID® Express uses our patented code generation process to enable users to generate and download unique psID® codes via a user-friendly, secure web portal.

Supply Chain

psID® Repo, our L4 – L5 Solution is a cloud-based data repository for requesting codes, creating T3 Documents, and querying past transactions. Any data required by the national regulatory authorities are stored and communicated through psID® Repo.

psID® Trail is a flexible track-and-trace portal that offers manufacturers and external partners, such as retailers and dealers, a seamless way to maintain control over the movement of products in the supply chain.

psID® Trail Guardian is our restricted distribution mobile application across Android & iOS platforms for field force verification of products. It helps in product tracking, dashboard and analytics to give you complete information about any code.

psID® Trail Desktop is a customized application which works as a track and trace software. Using this application dispatch of consignment can be achieved.
